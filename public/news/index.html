Centrifuge meets... Dharma

Centrifuge and Dharma hosted an evening to showcase the integration of their systems to enable an open, decentralized financial system. Centrifuge is enabling its users to tokenize off-chain documents and register them on the blockchain while maintaining privacy of the underlying data. Integrating with Dharma allows businesses to use these tokenized assets to access better, decentralized funding through Dharma's flexible underwriting and relaying protocols.
